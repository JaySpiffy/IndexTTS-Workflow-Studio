# Dark Garden Parody

This version is darker, more theatrical, and rewritten for the newer v2 scripting guidance.

Replace the placeholder labels in this pack, such as `SpeakerOne` and `SpeakerSix`, with the exact voice labels from your own `Available Voices` list before you generate it.

## Why This Version Is Better For V2

- shorter lines
- cleaner pauses
- less punctuation spam
- more room for timeline pacing later

## Pasteable Script

```text

SpeakerSix: Dress up the poor with petals, and suddenly cutting them down sounds tasteful.

SpeakerTwo: Taxonomy helps.
SpeakerTwo: Roses for capital. Orchids for celebrity. Dandelions for the crowded excess.
SpeakerTwo: Rename the lower beds as weeds, and culling starts to sound like maintenance.

SpeakerFour: That is one of the creepiest things I have ever heard.
SpeakerFour: And I have talked to a lot of guys who hunt enlightenment.

SpeakerSix: The hedge roses get chandeliers and guarded gates.

SpeakerTwo: Then the mass grave starts to look like landscaping.

SpeakerOne: Stay calm.

SpeakerSix: There is a sadness in the room.
SpeakerSix: I do not like it.
SpeakerSix: Find it.

SpeakerTwo: Heat bloom detected.
SpeakerTwo: The spy is having a conscience event.

SpeakerOne: Oh no.
SpeakerOne: They can hear the fear.

SpeakerFour: Is that guy whispering himself into a breakdown?

SpeakerOne: Human lives are not props.
SpeakerOne: They had names. Bad jokes. Favorite meals. Half-finished errands.
SpeakerOne: If you call them flowers long enough, you forget they screamed.

SpeakerSix: He is crying, but he is making points.
SpeakerSix: That is the problem.
```
